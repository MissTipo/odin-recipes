Ingredients

  • chicken
  • cheese
  • enchillada sauce
  • tortillas

Steps

  1. Slow cook chicken until tender
  2. Shred the slow coocked chicken
  3. place a torilla on a pate and add shredded chicken
  4. fold the tortilla and put in a baking trey
  5. Pour the enchilada sauce on top on the rolled enchiladas in the trey
  6. Cover with grated cheese
  7. Bake in a preheated oven for 15 minutes or until the cheese melts
